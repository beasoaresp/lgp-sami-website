Meet the Team!

We are a team of thirteen Master's students from the University of Porto, developed within the "Project Management Laboratory" course. Our goal is to foster a world where physical disability is no longer a barrier to musical expression. With SAMI, a mobile application that uses eye and face tracking, blowing sensors and small finger movements to control parameters to generate music.
